about us

the best Interior Design in the city

Frameworkz was created as a one stop solution company for all Project Management and related services in the year 2009 with endless dreams and potential possibilities only to scale up every year. By mid-2010, Frameworkz leaped into design consultancy providing design solutions to India's most of the biggest corporate houses. Impressed by the company's professionalism, one of the clientele suggested to think out of the box and step into execution as well.

Frameworkz gave it a shot and after much research and deliberation, in 2011, it started providing Turnkey solutions as well. Facility Management soon followed as the final business vertical, and now the concept of Design - Create - Manage has been imbibed into the roots.

our services

Design Consultancy

Our design consultancy service is tailored to provide you with the best design solutions for your space. Whether you're seeking a fresh perspective, expert advice, or comprehensive design strategies, our team of seasoned professionals is dedicated to understanding your unique needs and translating them into innovative design concepts that elevate your space to new heights of style and functionality.

Project Management

From inception to completion, our project management service ensures every aspect of your interior design project is meticulously planned, prepared, and executed with precision. We handle every detail, from initial consultations and evaluations to final layout decisions, guiding you through the process with expertise and transparency to bring your vision to life seamlessly.

Turnkey Solutions

Our turnkey solutions offer a comprehensive approach to interior design, covering every aspect of the process from start to finish. Whether you're opening a new location or revitalizing an existing space, we handle everything from site selection and negotiations to space planning and construction coordination. With our expertise and attention to detail, we transform your vision into reality, delivering turnkey solutions that exceed expectations
